About Me


Career Goals

Hello, I'm Ryan. My goal is to get into the video game development industry. Ever since I was a kid, video games have always been a great way to relax and unwind, or explore a different world. I would love to help create games to help bring that joy to others.


Professional Background

I spent 5 years working as a technical writer, developing SOPs and other large documents that required a lot of detail for training purposes and legal compliance. I've also worked as an auto mechanic for several years and I'm currently employed as a property manager/building maintenance technician. These career paths were very different from one another, but they created a unique foundation for learning to code. The detail-oriented work required for technical writing gave me a lot of experience following technical guidelines, in much the same way that I've had to follow along with coding projects in Codecademy and expect to see in the field. The troubleshooting and root cause analysis skills that I've learned as a mechanic and maintenance worker have also served me well, as I think of code as a mechanical system where different lines of code work as cogs in the overall machine.
